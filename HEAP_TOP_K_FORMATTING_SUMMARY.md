## Key Concepts Covered

### Heap Selection Strategy:
- **Max Heap for K Closest**: Keep farthest of k closest at top
- **Min Heap for K Largest**: Keep smallest of k largest at top
- **Min Heap for Merging**: Always extract global minimum

### Complexity Patterns:
- K Closest Points: O(n log k)
- Merge K Lists: O(N log k) where N = total nodes
- Kth Largest: O(n log k)
- Kth Smallest Matrix: O(k log n)

### Alternative Approaches:
- QuickSelect: O(n) average for selection problems
- Sorting: O(n log n) simple but slower
- Binary Search: O(n log(max-min)) for matrix problems
